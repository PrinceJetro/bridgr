Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a transfer take? expand_more

Most transfers arrive within 1-3 business days. Direct institutional payments are often faster (same day in some cases).

What's the minimum/maximum amount? expand_more

Minimum: £10 / $15 / ₦5,000. Maximum depends on your verification level (Standard: $50k, Premium: Unlimited).

Is it safe? Are you regulated? expand_more

Yes. We're FCA-regulated in the UK, partner with CBN-licensed institutions in Nigeria, and hold all funds in ring-fenced accounts. PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant.

How does the Fair Fee Shield work? expand_more

When you confirm your transfer, we lock the exchange rate. If market volatility exceeds 2% in the next 24 hours, your recipient's amount is protected at the locked rate.

Can I use Bridgr as a business? expand_more

Yes! We have special business tiers with volume discounts, batch processing, and dedicated support. Contact sales@bridgr.com.
